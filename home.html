What is Coworking?

Coworking is a concept that brings together professionals from different areas in the same collective office. The model emerged in the United States and its main purpose is the sharing of experiences and the mix of skills as the fuel for partnerships and innovations. The Link2u open space encourages interaction between people. Our environment facilitates the exchange of experiences, new business, visibility and networking.

On those times that you need a neat idea or some advice, there's nothing better than to look to the side and poke the neighbor to see what he thinks.
