About Link2U

The Link2U is much more than a workspace. It is a place that brings together different professionals with different backgrounds that share the same values.

We follow the coworking and shared office model where a social, collaborative and informal atmosphere prevail.

Our goal is to be a place where people can come to interact, use tools that encourage this process and find specialists from different areas at a "poke" away.

What is a Coworking Shared Office?

In Brazil, the coworking or shared office is still something unknown and unheralded by most. But this type of shared and collaborative working is gaining more space in the business environment.

It is increasingly common to find professionals who have chosen to work independently, either as contractors, consultants, freelancers or even small businesses in the embryonic stage, which are in the coworking model solution to combine low operating costs and excellent infrastructure in an interactive, stimulating and creative environment.

In many places the coworking is already an established trend, in Brazil this is also becoming true. Each week more and more spaces are created and each day, more professionals change their lives by changing their current traditional job for a lifestyle that values ​​freedom without compromising their professional relationships.

How about trying a new way to relate to your work and completely change your patterns of the corporate world?

The space costs little, is open doors and full of interesting people. Tempting, isn't it?
